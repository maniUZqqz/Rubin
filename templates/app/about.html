About us

Rubikamp's commitment is to create a leading online institution to nurture the next generation of talented youth technology leaders and entrepreneurs. Motivated, pragmatic and skilled teenagers who shape the future by solving real-world challenges. Rubikamp is a community of like-minded and goal-oriented teenagers and experienced and thoughtful people in the field of technology from all over the world who work together to raise the next generation. Rubikamp professors and mentors are from leading companies and universities in Iran and the world to make sure that Rubikamp teenagers get knowledge that is up-to-date and in line with the needs of leading companies. The educational path of Rubikamp, such as artificial intelligence, digital marketing, programming and entrepreneurship, has always been evolving and revised so that all educational items are integrated to increase efficiency and in line with the cultivation of a mentality of an efficient person and leader. We are committed to shaping the future of technology leaders and entrepreneurs, helping them push the boundaries and build a better future for everyone, regardless of their circumstances. read more
